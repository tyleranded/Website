Hello!

This is just a simple online portfolio of things that I do, places I go and sites I see! When a grad student gets bored at home, he learns HTML and CSS on his own with Bootstrap capabilities, editing photos with Adobe Photoshop, videos in Adobe Premiere Pro, and using Dreamweaver and CoffeeCup software.

My next and current adventures are going to Grad School for my Masters of Science in Communication Management at Towson University, of which I also received my Bachelor's of Science in Communication Studies. In a few weeks, I'll be traveling to Peru where I'll be visiting Lima, Cusco and Arequipa. Of course I'll be seeing Macchu Picchu!

I'm not quite as much of a traveler as some of my colleagues, but I try my best to go somewhere big once a year. I've traveled alone, but recently have been traveling with 1-2 other people. I think it's a great way to experience life and get to know strangers.
